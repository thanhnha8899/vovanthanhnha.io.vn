# Hoc8.com Exploration Progress

## Findings
- **Purpose**: AI platform for Vietnamese teachers to create lesson plans, creative educational initiatives (SKKN), and other teaching materials.
- **Type**: Educational service / SaaS for teachers.
- **Layout**: Single-page modern layout with sections for hero/login, products, company info, and policies.
- **Design Elements**:
    - **Header**: Sticky/fixed header with logo (HOC8.COM), navigation links (Sản phẩm, Doanh nghiệp, Chính sách), and contact phone.
    - **Hero Section**: Left side has benefits and branding; right side has a login form (email/password).
    - **Products Section**: Grid layout with cards for "Giáo án Mầm non", "KHBD 3 Cột (2345)", "Sáng kiến kinh nghiệm (SKKN)".
    - **Color Palette**: 
        - Primary: Teal/Cyan (buttons, highlights, icons).
        - Primary Text: Dark Slate/Blue (headings, main text).
        - Background: White with light blue/grey gradients in some sections.
        - Footer: Dark Navy/Charcoal background.
        - Accents: Red/Pink for point values in cards.
    - **Typography**: Modern sans-serif (clean, professional). Bold for headings, regular for body text.
    - **Interactive Elements**:
        - Hover effects: Header and footer links change color to teal.
        - Smooth scroll: Navigation buttons scroll to specific page sections.
    - **Footer**: Three columns (Logo/About, Links, Contact) + Ministry of Industry and Trade certification badge.
